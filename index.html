Scientists have discovered a massive black hole

Scientists have discovered a new Chernau hole, which is 70 times more massive than the Sun. Previously, scientists believed that the mass of a stellar black hole could not be 30 times the mass of the Sun. So what is it? A new discovery forcing scientists to rethink their models of black hole formation, or an unknown error in the study?

NASA has created an ice map of ice on Mars

NASA has big plans to return the astronauts to the moon in 2024, this will be a stepping stone to sending people to Mars. But where should the first people land on the Red Planet? A new article published in Geophysical Research Letters will help by providing a map of water ice, which is believed to be only 2.5 centimeters below the surface.

A huge new storm forms a hexagon at the South Pole of Jupiter

SAN FRANCISCO — NASA's Juno probe discovered a giant new storm swirling near Jupiter's south pole last month, a few weeks after pulling off a dramatic death-dodging maneuver. Juno spied the newfound maelstrom, which is about as wide as Texas, on Nov. 3, during its most recent close flyby of Jupiter.
